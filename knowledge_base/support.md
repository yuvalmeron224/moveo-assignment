## Reservation Policy

- A reservation holds a vehicle for **72 hours** and reduces the available stock count.
- Reservations are free of charge and require no deposit.
- After 72 hours, the reservation expires automatically and the stock is released.
- You may cancel a reservation at any time with no penalty by contacting support.

## Refund & Cancellation Policy

### Before Delivery
- If you cancel your purchase before vehicle delivery, you receive a **full refund** of any deposit paid.
- Cancellations must be submitted in writing to support@premiumcars.com.
- Processing time: 3–5 business days.

### After Delivery
- **Within 7 days / 500 miles:** Full refund, no questions asked. Vehicle must be returned in original condition.
- **8–30 days:** Store credit only, equivalent to 90% of purchase price.
- **After 30 days:** No refund. Standard warranty support applies.

### Non-Refundable Items
- Registration and title fees are non-refundable once submitted to the DMV.
- Optional add-ons (tinting, protection packages) are non-refundable after installation.

## Dispute Resolution

If you have a complaint or dispute:
1. Contact our Customer Relations team at relations@premiumcars.com.
2. We will acknowledge your complaint within 1 business day.
3. A resolution will be proposed within 5 business days.
4. If unresolved, disputes are handled through binding arbitration in Los Angeles County, CA.

## Purchase Intent & Email Communication

When you express intent to purchase a vehicle and provide your email address, our sales team will contact you within 2 business hours with a formal purchase agreement and next steps.
