# Frequently Asked Questions

## Test Drives

### How do I schedule a test drive?
You can schedule a test drive through three channels:
- **Online:** Use the booking form on our website (24/7 availability)
- **Phone:** Call +1 (800) 555-0199 during business hours
- **In-Person:** Walk in during showroom hours — same-day test drives are usually available

### What do I need to bring for a test drive?
- A valid driver's license (must not be expired or suspended)
- Proof of insurance (your existing personal auto insurance covers test drives)
- You must be at least 21 years old

### How long is a test drive?
Standard test drives are **30 minutes**. For vehicles priced above $100,000, extended 60-minute test drives are available by appointment.

### Can I take an extended overnight test drive?
Yes, for pre-approved customers purchasing vehicles above $80,000. A refundable $500 hold is placed on your credit card. The vehicle must be returned with no additional mileage beyond 150 miles.

### Can multiple people test drive the same vehicle?
Yes, but each driver must present a valid license and be listed on your insurance policy.

## Financing & Payment

### What payment methods do you accept?
- Bank wire transfer
- Certified cashier's check
- Dealer-arranged financing (OAC)
- We do **not** accept personal checks or cash payments above $10,000 (per federal reporting requirements)

### Do you offer financing?
Yes. We work with multiple preferred lenders. Terms range from 24 to 84 months. Credit approval typically takes 30–60 minutes during business hours.

### Can I get pre-approved before visiting?
Yes. Submit a pre-approval request online or via phone. Pre-approvals are valid for 30 days.

## Vehicle Information

### Are your vehicles certified pre-owned?
All pre-owned vehicles undergo a 150-point inspection. Vehicles with fewer than 25,000 miles and model year 2022+ may qualify for manufacturer CPO programs depending on the brand.

### Can I see the vehicle history report?
Yes. A full Carfax or AutoCheck report is available for every pre-owned vehicle, at no charge.

### Do you accept trade-ins?
Yes. See our Trade-In Policy for details. Bring your vehicle's title, current registration, and any service records.

## Delivery

### Do you offer home delivery?
Yes. See our Shipping & Delivery policy for full details on zones, timelines, and fees.

### Can I pick up my vehicle at the dealership?
Yes. Dealership pickup is always available and free of charge. Allow 1–2 hours for paperwork and vehicle orientation.
