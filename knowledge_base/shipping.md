# Shipping & Delivery Policy

## Delivery Zones & Timeframes

### Zone 1 — Local (Within 50 miles of Beverly Hills, CA)
- **Cost:** Free
- **Timeframe:** 1–2 business days after purchase confirmation
- **Method:** Enclosed transport truck, white-glove delivery to your address

### Zone 2 — Regional (50–500 miles)
- **Cost:** $299
- **Timeframe:** 3–5 business days
- **Method:** Enclosed transport truck

### Zone 3 — Continental US (500+ miles)
- **Cost:** $599
- **Timeframe:** 7–14 business days
- **Method:** Enclosed transport truck (preferred) or open carrier for vehicles under $50,000

### Zone 4 — Hawaii & Alaska
- **Cost:** $1,499
- **Timeframe:** 14–21 business days
- **Method:** Sea freight + enclosed ground transport

### International Delivery
- Currently not available. We do not ship outside the United States.

## Delivery Process

### Step 1 — Purchase Confirmation
After your purchase is finalized and payment cleared, you will receive a confirmation email with your delivery tracking number within 24 hours.

### Step 2 — Vehicle Preparation
Your vehicle undergoes final inspection, detailing, and documentation preparation. This takes 1 business day.

### Step 3 — Dispatch
The vehicle is loaded onto an enclosed carrier. You receive an SMS notification with the carrier's name and contact number.

### Step 4 — In-Transit Updates
You will receive daily SMS updates with the vehicle's location. Real-time tracking is available via our online portal.

### Step 5 — Delivery Appointment
Our delivery team will call you 24 hours before arrival to confirm your delivery window (2-hour windows).

### Step 6 — White-Glove Handover
Our delivery specialist will:
- Walk you through all vehicle features (30–60 minutes)
- Ensure you are set up with the manufacturer's app (e.g., Tesla app, MyBMW)
- Hand over all keys, documents, and warranty booklets
- Perform a joint inspection and have you sign the delivery receipt

---

## Delivery Requirements

- An adult (18+) must be present to sign the delivery receipt.
- Delivery to a business address is accepted — ensure someone is available to receive.
- If no one is present at the scheduled time, a redelivery fee of $150 applies.

## Vehicle Damage During Transport

- All vehicles are insured during transport at full replacement value.
- Inspect the vehicle thoroughly before signing the delivery receipt.
- Any transport damage noted at delivery will be repaired at no cost within 10 business days.
- Damage reported after signing the delivery receipt cannot be attributed to transport.

## Expedited Delivery

Expedited delivery (within Zone 1–2) is available for an additional $499 fee. Contact support within 2 hours of purchase confirmation to request expedited service. Availability is not guaranteed.
