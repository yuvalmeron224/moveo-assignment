# Sales & Inventory Policy

## Vehicle Eligibility Policy

Our dealership exclusively sells vehicles from **model year 2022 or newer**.

Any vehicle with a model year of 2021 or earlier is classified as **"Pending De-listing"**. These vehicles:
- Cannot be sold, reserved, or delivered under any circumstances
- Remain visible in our inventory system for administrative purposes only
- Must not be offered to customers as available for purchase

This policy applies regardless of the vehicle's condition, mileage, or stock availability.

## Condition Standards

All vehicles sold by our dealership must meet the following standards:

- **New vehicles (0 mileage):** Factory fresh, full manufacturer warranty active.
- **Pre-owned vehicles:** Maximum 70,000 miles. Full vehicle history report (Carfax) provided.
- All vehicles undergo a 150-point inspection before being listed as available.
- Any vehicle failing inspection is removed from the active inventory automatically.

## Warranty Policy

- New vehicles carry the full original manufacturer warranty.
- Pre-owned vehicles (under 25,000 miles) receive an additional 12-month / 15,000-mile dealership warranty.
- Pre-owned vehicles (25,000–70,000 miles) receive a 6-month / 7,500-mile powertrain warranty.
- Warranty is non-transferable and applies to the original purchasing customer only.

## Pricing Policy

- All listed prices are final and include standard dealer preparation fees.
- Prices do not include applicable taxes, registration fees, or optional add-ons.
- Price adjustments may occur without notice. The price at time of reservation confirmation is the binding price.

## Trade-In Policy

- We accept trade-ins for vehicles model year 2018 or newer in good condition.
- Trade-in valuations are valid for 7 days from the date of appraisal.
- Trade-in value is applied as a direct discount against the purchase price.
